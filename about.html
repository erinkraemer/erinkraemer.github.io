Erin Kraemer

Human-Centered Human with a Passion for Technology

Hello hi! It's nice to meet you.

I'm a recent Computer Science graduate (August 2022) from UC Berkeley. I was fortunate to study the absolute coolest subdomain of CS, Human Computer Interaction.

I'm endlessly curious about the ways that technology can better benefit humankind. I'm a champion for interventions of at all levels of maturity: everything from novel innovation to iterating on and pivoting seemingly immobile, longstanding structures and systems.

I like working within multifaceted problem spaces with seemingly opposing stakeholders. I believe that there is always a sensible intervention that maximizing the goals of all stakeholders. I love finding that common ground works maximally works for all stakeholders.

I like to work on problems with no objectively right answer. But, whatever problem I'm solving, human-ness remains the focal point. I like to learn how other people percieve the world around them and I like learning the pieces that uncover those things.

I love being told that I am wrong. The world is constantly changing and I can only see the world from my view. Being told I'm wrong and altering my worldview is one of the greatest gifts a person can give.

Frankly, I want to find jobs that fit me. I like projects and so do companies.
