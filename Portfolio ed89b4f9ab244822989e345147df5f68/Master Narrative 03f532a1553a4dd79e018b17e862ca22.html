The pandemic was in full swing. The vaccination efforts with wildly successful within certain populations.

But this was not true for all populations.

Because of the focus on efficency rather than equity, the internet was over-utilized in the initial distribution. This meant that the people most vulnerable to the virus including low income older adults without internet skills and access were unable to access the vaccine.

I knew many internet-savvy people that were eager to help but did not know how. That’s why my honors Fung fellowship team and I made Shotline.
